# 🐱 CatZero OS

An advanced, open-source 2.4GHz, Sub-GHz, and Infrared cybersecurity development platform. CatZero is built specifically to bridge the gap between low-frequency hardware hacking and aggressive, modern wireless security auditing. 

Unlike consumer multi-tools that lock capabilities behind restricted firmware, CatZero is designed by the community, for the community—engineered to turn standard microcontrollers into weaponized testing units.

## ⚡ Why CatZero Overrides the Competition

While commercial alternatives like the Flipper Zero offer broad convenience, they suffer from severe hardware limitations that cripple their effectiveness in real-world wireless engagements. CatZero leaves them behind in three key areas:

### 1. Weaponized 2.4GHz Operations (The Flipper Killer)
* **The Competition's Flaw:** Out of the box, standard tools have **zero native Wi-Fi** capabilities and a highly restricted, low-energy Bluetooth architecture mainly used for smartphone app syncing. To run a basic Wi-Fi test, users must buy, wire, and carry bulky external daughterboards.
* **The CatZero Advantage:** Powered natively by a dual-core 240MHz processor with an dedicated external antenna connection, CatZero handles high-speed 2.4GHz traffic directly. It executes advanced multi-target Wi-Fi deauthentication, massive BLE proximity spamming, and hosts reactive Evil Portals completely standalone.

### 2. Proximity Bluetooth Exploitation (`BLE Suite`)
* **The Competition's Flaw:** Modern smartphone operating systems easily block or ignore the basic Bluetooth advertising packets sent by older hardware toolsets.
* **The CatZero Advantage:** CatZero features a fully integrated **BLE Suite**. It moves past simple prank popup spam to provide real recon, full service enumeration, and proof-of-concept testing for critical protocols (such as HFP state confusion exploits).

### 3. Streamlined 1-Button Tactical Interface
* **The Competition's Flaw:** Bulky 5-way D-pads and plastic buttons increase the physical footprint of a device, making it obvious and difficult to deploy discreetly.
* **The CatZero Advantage:** Utilizing an elegant **1-Button UI System** (Short press to scroll, Long press to execute/go back), CatZero squeezes a massive nested menu architecture into an ultra-compact frame. It delivers high utility with minimal hardware vulnerability.

## ⚔️ Armed Attack Capabilities

CatZero organizes its offensive toolsets into four dedicated tactical modules, navigated seamlessly through high-contrast animated protocol loops:

### 📡 Wi-Fi Pentesting Suite
* **Karma & AP Spoofing:** Broadcasts trusted network probe responses to mimic familiar routers, forcing target devices to connect automatically.
* **Multi-Protocol Beacon Spam:** Floods the airwaves with thousands of fake networks (including custom SSIDs and randomized arrays) to blind wireless scanners.
* **Advanced Target Flooding:** Precision station deauthentication and automated ARP poisoning to intercept traffic on local networks.
* **Evil Portals:** Spins up instantaneous, captive credential-harvesting pages for phishing verification and access testing.

### 💙 BLE Suite (Bluetooth Low Energy)
* **Multi-OS Proximity Flood:** Blasts targeted, protocol-compliant pairing requests across Android (FastPair), iOS (AirPods/AppleTV pairing loops), and Windows (SwiftPair) simultaneously.
* **HFP & Audio Hijacking:** Features an integrated framework to test Hands-Free Profile vulnerabilities, audio stack crashing, and AVRCP remote control injection.
* **HID Over BLE:** Functions as a wireless injection platform, feeding rapid DuckyScript keystrokes directly into targeted devices over encrypted Bluetooth connections.

### 📻 Sub-GHz Radio Subsystem
* **ProtoPirate Automotive Engine:** A powerful 11-protocol automotive decoder capable of intercepting, parsing, and rolling-code transmitting signals for vehicles (including Kia, Chrysler, Jeep, StarLine, Ford, Toyota, and more).
* **Signal Jamming & Brute-Forcing:** Offers both full-band and intermittent tactical RF jamming alongside automated protocol brute-forcing routines.
* **Visual Spectrum Analyzer:** Renders real-time RSSI spectrograms and square-wave readouts directly to the display for rapid frequency discovery.

### 👁️ Infrared Control Module
* **TV-B-Gone / Total Blackout:** Rapid-cycles through hundreds of universal power codes to instantly neutralize display walls, TVs, and digital signage.
* **IR Read, Replay & Jammer:** Captures proprietary consumer remote signatures, saves them locally, and blasts them back with amplified IR diodes.
* **IR2Keyboard:** Low-profile pivoting script that translates standard remote presses into direct PC keyboard commands.
